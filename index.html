About SoundBooster

SoundBooster is a lightweight Windows audio utility designed to safely increase system volume beyond standard limits while maintaining sound clarity and stability. It works by routing audio through a virtual audio engine, allowing controlled volume amplification with built-in protection against distortion, clipping, and potential driver damage. SoundBooster includes smart safety warnings, an AI-assisted equalizer for improved sound quality at high boost levels, and a clean, intuitive interface. The software features both Pro View and Mini View, runs quietly in the background, and remembers user preferences such as window size, theme, and audio settings. Installation and uninstallation are fully automated, including required audio drivers, ensuring a clean system experience. Frequently Asked Questions

Why is it free?
Me personally, as the developer, I always loved music but didn't like the sound of it on standard setups. Other softwares like to be greedy with high prices; I wanted to create something powerful that everyone can use for free.
What is VB-CABLE and is it safe?
VB-CABLE is a virtual audio device that works as a virtual audio cable. It is 100% safe and widely used by audio professionals. SoundBooster uses it to "catch" your system audio so it can be amplified before reaching your speakers.
Is it safe for my speakers?
Yes. SoundBooster includes an AI Anti-Clip engine and real-time safety warnings when you exceed 250% boost to help you avoid hardware damage.
Does it work with all Windows apps?
Absolutely. Once the engine is activated, it boosts all system audio, including Chrome, Spotify, YouTube, and Games.
Which OS does it support right now?
SoundBooster currently supports Windows 10 and Windows 11 (64-bit).
Why doesn't it support macOS or Linux?
The current audio engine and VB-CABLE integration are built specifically for the Windows Audio Architecture. While a macOS version is being considered, the priority is perfecting the Windows experience first.
